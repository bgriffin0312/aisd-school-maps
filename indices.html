Move burden — is it equitable?

Share of each group whose block group is reassigned (current → illustrative). A redraw is only fair if no group is asked to move far more than others.

Every ratio is between 0.86 and 1.13 — within the campaign's "no group more than 1.25×" standard, versus 2.34× for Black high schoolers under Scenario 14. The redraw is large (~60% of students move) but the burden is shared roughly evenly across groups.

▼ lower is better  ·  ▲ higher is better. Best value per row in red. Dissimilarity Index = % of a group that would need to switch schools for an even distribution. Exposure = share of a typical student's schoolmates who are White.

Texas Appleseed analysis. AISD attendance-boundary shapefiles; ACS 2018–22; TEA 2024–25. The illustrative scenario includes a McCallum→Travis fine-arts magnet relocation that lifts Travis to a feasible utilization while McCallum backfills from its zone.
